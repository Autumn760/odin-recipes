Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal

Apple cinnamon oatmeal is a delicious and nutritious breakfast dish that combines the heartiness of oats with the sweet and warm flavors of apples and cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup apple juice
  • 1 apple, sliced or diced
  • 2/3 cup rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Combine the water, apple juice, and apples in a saucepan.
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat, and stir in the rolled oats and cinnamon.
  3. eturn to a boil, then reduce heat to low, and simmer until thick, about 3 minutes.
  4. Spoon into serving bowls, and pour milk over the servings.
