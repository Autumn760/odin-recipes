Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal

Apple cinnamon oatmeal is a delicious and nutritious breakfast dish that combines the heartiness of oats with the sweet and warm flavors of apples and cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 apple, sliced or diced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Combine rolled oats and water/milk in a pot.
  2. Add sliced or diced apples.
  3. Stir in ground cinnamon.
